Dr. Pragna Sagar Rapole

Consultant Radiation Oncologist CARE Hospitals, Hyderabad

Dr. Pragna Sagar Rapole was trained at Jawaharlal Institute of Post-Graduate Medical Education and Research, which stands as one among the best institutes in India.

Dr Sagar has a comprehensive clinical experience which he accumulated from his association with two Regional Cancer Centres equipped with conformal precision radiotherapy and brachytherapy services.

One of the most proficient and a well-known radiation oncology specialist in Hyderabad, Dr Pragna Sagar, holds exceptional expertise in the latest radiation oncology techniques like 3D-CRT, IMRT, IGRT, SRS, SBRT, brachytherapy for cancers.

He has special interest in treatment of Lung cancer, Uterine cancer, Brain tumors and childhood cancers.
