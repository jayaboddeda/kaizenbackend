Dr. Satish Pawar

Senior Consultant and Head of Surgical oncology - CARE Hospitals, Hyderabad

Dr. Satish Pawar, an experienced and a proficient Oncology Surgeon, has close to a decade of experience. His expertise extends from advanced Laparoscopic surgeries in surgical oncology such as Lap Wertheims, Lap primary and Interval Cytoreduction, to Lap AR/APR/Hemicolectomy, and Lap Radical Nephrectomy/ Cystectomy/ Gastrectomy/ Esophagectomy Lung (VATS).

Dr, Pawar is also a dexterous surgeon in nerve sparing Laparoscopic Radical Hysterectomy, Sphincter preserving minimally invasive rectal surgeries, Single Port VATS Lobectomy with Mediastinal lymph node dissection, and TaTME for low rectal cancers.

Email: drsatish@kaizenoncology.com
