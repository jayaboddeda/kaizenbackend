Dr. Sudha Sinha, MD

Clinical Director, Oncology Services and Head of Department, Medical Oncology, CARE HOSPITALS, Hyderabad.

Dr. Sudha Sinha is currently the Clinical Director, Oncology Services and Head of department, Medical Oncology at Care Hospitals, Hyderabad.

She graduated from Gandhi Medical College, Hyderabad, India and went on to complete her MD in Internal Medicine from St Barnabas Medical Center, Livingston, New Jersey, USA (Part of Mount Sinai Medical Center). She subsequently completed her DM (Fellowship) in Hematology/Oncology from St Elizabeth's Medical Center, Tufts University School of Medicine.

Dr. Sudha is certified by American board of Hematology, American board of Oncology and American board of Internal Medicine. She joined as Assistant Professor at MNJ Institute of Oncology and Regional Cancer Center, Hyderabad in 2007 as soon as she returned from USA. She had a distinguished career at MNJ, where she was Head of Medical Oncology Department before she moved into Private sector in 2017.
