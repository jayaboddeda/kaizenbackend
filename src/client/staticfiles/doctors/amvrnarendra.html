Prof Dr. A.M.V.R. Narendra

MBBS, MD, DM - Sr. Consultant Hematology & BMT

Prof Dr. Anukonda Moti Venkata Raja Narendra holds over 2 decades of experience in the field of Haematology and Hemato-Oncology.

Being one of the finest haematologists in Hyderabad, he offers remarkable results when treating patients who are diagnosed with blood disorders like Myelodysplastic syndrome, Thalassemia, Congenital/Acquired bone marrow failure, Acute myeloid leukaemia, Aplastic Anaemia, Lymphoma, Sickle cell disease and many more such conditions.

He has a special interest towards Anti-thymocyte globulin therapy, Arsenic Trioxide therapy, and cell transplants. Bone marrow transplant is his speciality niche.

Email: drnarendra@kaizenoncology.com
